2i Multiples challenge

This Program iterates in multiples of A until X, then in multiples of A + 1 until 2X, then multiples of A + 2 until 3X.

**** This program Assumes you enter a positive numbers and A is less or equal to X ****

There are two Approaches in solving this challenge:

First: the multiples are generated considering the sequence





Second: the multiples are generated discarding the sequence
